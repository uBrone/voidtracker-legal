# 🛡️ Privacy Policy — Cephalon VoidTracker
**B. Softworks** • Origin System Informant

---

## 🛰️ 1. Data Collection
Your data privacy is critically important. VoidTracker collects only what is necessary for its operation:
- **Server/Channel IDs**: To know where to send configured alerts.
- **User IDs**: For Squad organization and honor systems.
- **Settings**: Language and access permissions.

## 🛠️ 2. Use of Information
Collected information is used exclusively to:
- Send mission alerts.
- Manage member entry and exit in squads.
- Validate VIP subscription status.
- Monitor bot stability (Technical logs).

## 🔒 3. Storage and Security
Your data is stored in a **private and encrypted MongoDB database**. We do not share your data with third parties.

## 🌐 4. Third-Party Services
The bot interacts with the following official platforms:
- **Discord API**: For messaging.
- **Warframe API**: For game data.
- **Top.gg**: Global statistics only (number of servers).

## 🗑️ 5. Data Retention and Deletion
- Monitoring data is deleted upon stopping the monitor.
- Squads and temporary data are deleted after **3 hours**.
- You can request total deletion by removing the bot.

## ⚖️ 6. Compliance (GDPR/LGPD)
We follow global best practices for data minimization and protection.
